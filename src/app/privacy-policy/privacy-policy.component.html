PRIVACY POLICY

The department of Port, Shipping and Aviation herby discloses out information gathering and dissemination practices of personal and non-personal information we collect when you visit our site.

Information collected from the users that can be used to personally identify the users of the website will not be disseminated or disclosed unless we are required by the law to do so, or if such action is necessary to

  • Protect and defend our rights and property
  • To protect the personal safety or property of others.
  • Your IP address may be used to help diagnose issues with our server and for administration purpose.
  • Information gathered anonymously regarding the demographic distribution of our visitors, date and time of the visits, browser information and other site-traffic statistics would be used by our team to improve the website and serve you more efficiently.
  • The login/registration process to out website requires transmission of personal data. As explicitly mentioned in the registration page, our team holds the right to verify your contact address, Connection details, email address and phone number thus provided, in order to ensure the prudent and secure use of the online services we have provided the public.
